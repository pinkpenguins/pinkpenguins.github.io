At Pink Penguins, we strive to assist with creating a healthy work environment for everyone -
not only by amplifying voices of those underrepresented in tech, but also by educating
the “decision makers” on recognizing and addressing the conscious and unconscious prejudices that hinder growth.
We believe that through education and spreading awareness we can increase understanding of and empathy for one another and the daily struggles we all face.
